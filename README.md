# Yum Or Yuck! 🍪

**The Gamified Cookie Inspector and Digital Literacy Tool**  

Check if a website’s cookies are “yummy” (safe) or “yucky” (risky) in a fun and interactive way.

---

## Why
Cookie banners often say things like:  
*“We use cookies to improve your experience.”*  
*“Accept all / Manage preferences.”*  

But most users don’t understand what happens behind the scenes when they click:  
- Cross-site tracking  
- Data profiling  
- Ad network sharing  
- Behavioral fingerprinting  

Legal text like “third-party processors”, “retention period”, or “legitimate interest” is confusing. Yum Or Yuck! bridges that interpretability gap—helping users make informed decisions about their digital privacy.

---

## Features
- **Privacy Scoring:** Clear, simple cookie risk score.  
- **Plain-English Summaries:** Explain complex legal and technical text in everyday language.  
- **Gamified Badges:** Make privacy analysis engaging and memorable.  
- **AI-Powered:** Uses Google Gemini API to analyze websites.  
- **Blogs & Glossories:** Layman explanations for complex cookie terms and legal jargon.
- **Optional Voice Feedback for Accessibility:** (Future) via ElevenLabs for human-like narration.  

---

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Streamlit  
- **AI Analysis:** Google Gemini API
